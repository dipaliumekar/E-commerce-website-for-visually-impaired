About Us

Our Story

New technology and techniques are implemented in Ecommerce websites to visually present information due to significant growth in shopping worldwide. However, there are accessibility issues with these strategies for individuals with disabilities. Therefore, creating e-commerce websites that will satisfy customers with disabilities of all ages is extremely crucial. About 36 million individuals are blind, and there are approximately 161 million people who are visually impaired. People who are blind find it difficult to select clothing with a variety of colors, which makes internet purchasing challenging. To help blind individuals, an e-commerce platform that can automatically identify colors and patterns in clothing has been developed. This system processes the voice output and requests input by clicking the button of their preferred options. Which will allow people to purchase their preferred things online? They will receive audio instructions, which they must listen to and follow.

Our Mission

Blind people are basically like everyone else, except they can't see. Is this scenario as straightforward in reality as it appears in our minds? As one of Envision's founders stated, we have created a world that benefits the majority. Individuals who differ from the norm, such as the visually handicapped, may confront challenges due to their unique characteristics. This essay tries to draw attention on the obstacles that the visually impaired experience just by living their lives and being the odd ones out.
